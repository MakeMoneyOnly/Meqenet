## 2. Advanced Financial Crime Prevention

### 2.1 Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Excellence

**Comprehensive AML Framework:**

- **Real-time Transaction Monitoring:** Machine learning algorithms detect suspicious patterns
  including:
  - Structuring (breaking large transactions into smaller amounts to avoid reporting thresholds)
  - Layering (complex chains of transactions to obscure money trails)
  - Integration (reintroducing laundered funds into legitimate financial systems)
  - Rapid fire transactions and velocity patterns
  - Round number patterns and unusual geographic flows

- **Advanced Sanctions Screening:** Multi-list screening against:
  - **OFAC SDN List:** US Treasury Specially Designated Nationals
  - **UN Consolidated List:** United Nations sanctions database
  - **EU Sanctions List:** European Union restrictive measures
  - **Ethiopian Sanctions:** National Bank of Ethiopia sanctions
  - **Custom Watch Lists:** Industry-specific risk databases

- **Suspicious Activity Reporting (SAR):** Automated generation and filing of reports to Ethiopian
  Financial Intelligence Center (FIC)

### 2.2 Know Your Customer (KYC) & Enhanced Due Diligence

**Fayda National ID Exclusive Integration:**

- **Government Database Integration:** Direct connection to Ethiopian National ID Registry
- **Biometric Verification:** Advanced facial recognition with liveness detection
- **Document Authentication:** AI-powered detection of forged documents with hologram validation
- **Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD):** Risk-based approach for high-value customers
- **Continuous Monitoring:** Ongoing customer screening throughout relationship lifecycle

**Identity Verification Levels:**

1. **Basic Verification:** Standard Fayda ID validation with name, date of birth, and ID number
   matching
2. **Enhanced Verification:** Biometric matching with facial recognition and document authentication
3. **Premium Verification:** Full biometric suite including fingerprint and voice recognition for
   high-value accounts

### 2.3 Politically Exposed Person (PEP) Detection

**Comprehensive PEP Screening:**

- **Ethiopian Government Officials:** Automated screening against government databases
- **International PEP Lists:** Coverage of foreign government officials and their associates
- **Beneficial Ownership:** Detection of indirect PEP relationships through business structures
- **Family Members:** Extended screening for PEP family members and close associates
- **Ongoing Monitoring:** Continuous updates as PEP status changes

## 5. AI/ML Governance & Algorithmic Fairness

### 5.1 Financial Model Bias Prevention

**Comprehensive Bias Detection Framework:**

- **Demographic Parity Testing:** Ensuring equal approval rates across protected demographic groups
- **Equalized Odds Validation:** Equal true positive and false positive rates across all groups
- **Individual Fairness:** Similar individuals receive similar outcomes regardless of protected
  attributes
- **Counterfactual Fairness:** Decisions remain the same in a counterfactual world without
  discrimination
- **Causal Fairness:** Preventing use of variables that are proxies for protected attributes

**Ethiopian Market Specific Considerations:**

- **Regional Fairness:** Equal treatment across all Ethiopian regions (Addis Ababa, Oromia, Amhara,
  etc.)
- **Gender Equality:** Ensuring fair treatment regardless of gender in credit and payment decisions
- **Age-Based Assessment:** Reasonable age-based risk assessment without discriminatory practices
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Consideration of Ethiopian cultural patterns in model design
- **Language Neutrality:** Fair treatment regardless of primary language (Amharic, Oromo, English,
  etc.)

### 5.2 Model Explainability & Transparency

**Explainable AI (XAI) Implementation:**

- **Feature Importance Analysis:** Clear explanation of factors influencing credit and payment
  decisions
- **Local Explanations:** Individual-level explanations for specific customer decisions
- **Global Model Behavior:** Overall model behavior understanding for regulators and stakeholders
- **Counterfactual Explanations:** "What would need to change for a different outcome" analysis
- **Natural Language Explanations:** Plain language explanations in Amharic and English

**Regulatory Transparency:**

- **Model Documentation:** Comprehensive documentation of all AI/ML models used in financial
  decisions
- **Performance Monitoring:** Continuous tracking of model accuracy, bias, and fairness metrics
- **Audit Trail:** Complete audit trail of model training, testing, and deployment decisions
- **Regulatory Reporting:** Regular reporting on AI governance to NBE and international regulators
- **External Validation:** Independent third-party validation of critical financial models

### 5.3 Continuous Model Monitoring

**Real-time Bias Detection:**

- **Statistical Parity:** Continuous monitoring of outcome rates across demographic groups
- **Calibration Monitoring:** Ensuring predicted probabilities match actual outcomes across groups
- **Threshold Analysis:** Regular analysis of decision thresholds for discriminatory impact
- **Performance Degradation:** Automated detection of model performance issues and bias drift
- **Alert Systems:** Real-time alerts for significant bias or fairness violations

**Automated Remediation:**

- **Model Retraining:** Automated retraining with bias-corrected datasets when issues detected
- **Fairness Constraints:** Built-in fairness constraints during model training and optimization
- **Human Override:** Human review and override capabilities for high-impact decisions
- **Stakeholder Notification:** Automated notification of regulators and stakeholders for bias
  issues
- **Corrective Actions:** Systematic implementation of corrective actions for bias remediation
